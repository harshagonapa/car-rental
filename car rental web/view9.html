MINI COOPER

The Mini Cooper S is known for its sporty performance and compact design. Here are typical specifications for the Mini Cooper S, though variations may exist depending on the model year and specific trim level:

    *Engine:*

  • 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine
  • Power output: Approximately 189 horsepower
  • Torque: Around 207 lb-ft

    *Performance:*

  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph (0-97 km/h) in around 6.5-7.0 seconds, depending on the transmission and other factors.
  • Top Speed: Typically around 145-155 mph (233-250 km/h), depending on the model year and configuration.

    *Transmission:*

  • Available with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (known as the Steptronic transmission).

    *Dimensions:*

  • Length: Approximately 151.9 inches (386 cm)
  • Width: Approximately 68 inches (173 cm)
  • Height: Approximately 55.7 inches (141.5 cm)
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 98.2 inches (249.4 cm)

    *Cargo Space:*

  • Cargo capacity behind the second row: Typically around 8.7 cubic feet (246 liters)
  • Maximum cargo capacity with seats folded: Varies depending on the configuration, but usually over 34 cubic feet (962 liters)

    *Fuel Economy:*

  • Fuel efficiency varies depending on the transmission and driving conditions.
  • Typically ranges from around 25-30 mpg (9.4-7.8 L/100km) in the city and 32-38 mpg (7.4-6.2 L/100km) on the highway.

    *Seating Capacity:*

  • - The Mini Cooper S typically seats up to four passengers, though some configurations may offer seating for five.

    *Features:*

  • Features can vary depending on the trim level and optional packages, but typically include:
  • Leatherette or cloth upholstery
  • Dual-zone automatic climate control
  • Infotainment system with touchscreen display
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • LED headlights and taillights
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • Performance brakes
  • Dynamic Stability Control (DSC)
  • Various customization options for exterior and interior styling
