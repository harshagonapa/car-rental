RANGE ROVER

The 2018 Range Rover is a luxury SUV known for its blend of elegance, off-road capability, and advanced technology. Below are typical specifications for the 2018 Range Rover, though variations may exist depending on the trim level and optional packages:

    *Engine Options:*

  • 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine:
  • Power output: Approximately 340 horsepower
  • Torque: Around 332 lb-ft
  • 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine:
  • Power output: Approximately 518 horsepower
  • Torque: Around 461 lb-ft
  • 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine (Autobiography and SVAutobiography models):
  • Power output: Approximately 557 horsepower
  • Torque: Around 516 lb-ft
  • 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 diesel engine (available in some markets):
  • Power output: Varies depending on the specific configuration.

    *Performance:*

  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph (0-97 km/h) times vary depending on the engine configuration and trim level, ranging from around 5.1 to 7.1 seconds.
  • Top Speed: Depending on the engine and trim, top speeds can range from approximately 130 to 155 mph (209 to 250 km/h).

    *Transmission:*

  • An eight-speed automatic transmission is typically standard across the lineup.

    *Dimensions:*

  • Length: Approximately 196.8 inches (499.9 cm)
  • Width: Approximately 87.4 inches (222.3 cm) including mirrors
  • Height: Approximately 72.2 inches (183.4 cm)
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 115 inches (292.1 cm)

    *Cargo Space:*

  • Cargo capacity behind the second row: Typically around 31.8 cubic feet (899 liters)
  • Maximum cargo capacity with seats folded: Varies depending on the configuration, but usually over 68.6 cubic feet (1,943 liters)

    *Fuel Economy:*

  • Fuel efficiency varies depending on the engine and drivetrain configuration.
  • Typically ranges from around 14-22 mpg (16.8-10.7 L/100km) in the city and 19-28 mpg (12.4-8.4 L/100km) on the highway.

    *Seating Capacity:*

  • The Range Rover typically seats up to five passengers, though higher trims may offer optional seating configurations.

    *Features:*

  • Features can vary significantly depending on the trim level and optional packages, but typically include:
  • Premium leather upholstery
  • Touchscreen infotainment system with navigation
  • Meridian audio system
  • Adaptive cruise control
  • Terrain Response system for off-road capability
  • Advanced driver assistance systems
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Power-operated tailgate
