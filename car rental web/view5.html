BMW X7

*Engine Options:*

  • The BMW X7 typically offers a range of engine options, including inline-six and V8 engines.
  • Specific engines may vary depending on the model year and region.

    *Performance:*

  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph (0-97 km/h) times vary depending on the engine configuration, but typically range from around 5.4 to 7.0 seconds.
  • Top Speed: Varies depending on the engine and trim level, but can exceed 130 mph (209 km/h) in many configurations. *Transmission:*
  • Most BMW X7 models feature an eight-speed automatic transmission.

    *Dimensions:*

  • Length: Approximately 203.3 inches (516 cm)
  • Width: Approximately 78.7 inches (199.9 cm)
  • Height: Approximately 71.1 inches (180.6 cm)
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 122.2 inches (310.5 cm)

    *Cargo Space:*

  • Cargo capacity behind the second row: Typically around 48.6 cubic feet (1,376 liters)
  • Maximum cargo capacity with seats folded: Generally over 90 cubic feet (2,549 liters)

    *Fuel Economy:*

  • Fuel efficiency varies depending on the engine and drivetrain configuration.
  • Typically ranges from around 17-22 mpg (13.8-10.7 L/100km) in the city and 22-28 mpg (10.7-8.4 L/100km) on the highway.

    *Seating Capacity:*

  • The BMW X7 typically seats up to seven passengers, though some configurations may have seating for six.

    *Features:*

  • Features can vary depending on the trim level and optional packages, but typically include:
  • Advanced infotainment systems with touchscreen displays
  • Premium audio systems
  • Leather upholstery
  • Advanced driver assistance systems
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Power-operated tailgate
  • Adaptive suspension
