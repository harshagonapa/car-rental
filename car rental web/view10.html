VOLVO

The Volvo XC90 is a luxury SUV known for its safety features, elegant design, and advanced technology. Here are typical specifications for the Volvo XC90:

    *Engine Options:*

  • T5: 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine
  • Power output: Around 250 horsepower
  • Torque: Approximately 258 lb-ft
  • T6: 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged inline-4 engine
  • Power output: Around 316 horsepower
  • Torque: Approximately 295 lb-ft
  • Recharge T8 Plug-in Hybrid: Combines the T6 engine with an electric motor
  • Total system power output: Around 400 horsepower
  • Torque: Approximately 472 lb-ft

    *Performance:*

  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph (0-97 km/h) times vary depending on the engine and drivetrain configuration, but typically range from around 5.9 to 7.5 seconds.
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to around 130-140 mph (209-225 km/h), depending on the model.

    *Transmission:*

  • All engines typically come with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

    *Dimensions:*

  • Length: Approximately 194.9 inches (495.3 cm)
  • Width: Approximately 84.3 inches (214 cm) including mirrors
  • Height: Approximately 69.9 inches (177.5 cm)
  • Wheelbase: Approximately 117.5 inches (298.5 cm)

    *Cargo Space:*

  • Cargo capacity behind the second row: Typically around 41.8 cubic feet (1,182 liters)
  • Maximum cargo capacity with seats folded: Varies depending on the configuration, but usually over 85.7 cubic feet (2,424 liters)

    *Fuel Economy:*

  • Fuel efficiency varies depending on the engine and drivetrain configuration.
  • Typically ranges from around 18-21 mpg (13-11 L/100km) in the city and 26-30 mpg (9-7.8 L/100km) on the highway for gasoline models.
  • For the Recharge T8 Plug-in Hybrid, fuel economy will depend on driving habits and the frequency of electric driving.

    *Seating Capacity:*

  • The Volvo XC90 typically seats up to seven passengers in three rows, though some configurations may offer seating for six.

    *Features:*

  • Features can vary depending on the trim level and optional packages, but typically include:
  • Sensus touchscreen infotainment system
  • Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility
  • Leather upholstery with premium finishes
  • Panoramic sunroof
  • Advanced driver assistance systems (Volvo's IntelliSafe)
  • Semi-autonomous driving capabilities (Pilot Assist)
  • Four-zone automatic climate control
  • Power-operated tailgate
  • Available air suspension for a smoother ride
