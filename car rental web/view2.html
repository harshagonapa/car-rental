MERCEDES BENZ

Mercedes-Benz CLE is a brand-new model, replacing both the C-Class and E-Class coupes and convertibles. It offers a range of exciting specifications across different categories:

*Engines and Performance:*

    * *Petrol:*

  • CLE 300: 2.0L turbocharged I4 with 255 hp and 295 lb-ft torque, 0-60 mph in 5.2 seconds (est.)
  • CLE 450: 3.0L turbocharged I6 with 375 hp and 369 lb-ft torque, 0-60 mph in 4.2 seconds (est.)
  • AMG CLE 53: 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 with 449 hp and 600 Nm torque, 0-60 mph in 4.2 seconds

    • *Diesel:*

  • CLE 220d: 2.0L OM654 mild hybrid I4 with 204 hp, 0-60 mph in 7.4 seconds (est.)
  • All engines are mild-hybrids with a 48-volt system, adding 23 hp and 148 lb-ft torque.
  • 9-speed automatic transmission standard on all models.

    *Dimensions and Design:*

  • Wheelbase: 112.8 inches
  • Length: 191.0 inches
  • Width: 73.2 inches
  • Height: 54.3 inches
  • Sleek and sporty coupe design with optional convertible version available.
  • Panoramic sunroof standard on most models.

    *Interior and Technology:*

  • 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and 11.9-inch touchscreen MBUX infotainment system.
  • High-quality materials and finishes throughout the cabin.
  • Available features include ambient lighting, Burmester sound system, heads-up display, heated and ventilated seats, and massage function.
  • Driver assistance features include adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and blind spot monitoring.

    *Price and Release Date:*

  • Base price estimated at $60,000 for the CLE 300 and $75,000 for the CLE 450.
  • AMG CLE 53 pricing likely to start above $80,000.
  • The CLE is already available for order in some markets, with deliveries expected to begin in late 2023 or early 2024.
