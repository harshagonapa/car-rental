FERRARI

The Ferrari 812 GTS is a convertible variant of the Ferrari 812 Superfast, combining the high-performance characteristics of the Superfast with an open-top driving experience. Here are some key features and information about the Ferrari 812 GTS:

capacity 2

*Engine:*

  • The 812 GTS is powered by a 6.5-liter V12 engine, similar to the one found in the 812 Superfast.
  • It produces a remarkable 789 horsepower (588 kW) at 8,500 rpm and a peak torque of 718 Nm at 7,000 rpm.

    *Performance:*

  • The car boasts impressive performance figures, with a top speed exceeding 211 mph (340 km/h).
  • It can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph (0 to 100 km/h) in approximately 2.8 seconds.

    *Transmission:*

  • Equipped with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, providing quick and precise gear changes.

    *Convertible Top:*

  • The 812 GTS features a retractable hardtop, allowing the driver to enjoy the thrill of open-air driving.
  • The convertible top retracts or deploys in a relatively short amount of time, offering flexibility for different driving conditions.

    *Design:*

  • The design of the 812 GTS retains the aggressive and aerodynamic styling of the 812 Superfast.
  • The convertible design adds a touch of elegance, making it an attractive option for those who appreciate both performance and open-top driving.

    *Chassis and Handling:*

  • Like the 812 Superfast, the 812 GTS features a front-engine, rear-wheel-drive layout.
  • It incorporates advanced technologies such as 4-wheel steering and the Side Slip Control 5.0 system, enhancing stability and handling.

    *Weight:*

  • The curb weight of the Ferrari 812 GTS is approximately 1,790 kg.

    *Fuel Economy:*

  • The fuel consumption, as per the combined cycle, is around 13.8 L/100 km.

    *Technology:*

  • The car is equipped with various technologies, including the Ferrari Peak Performance (FPP) predictive performance optimization system.

    *Limited Production:*

  • Ferrari often produces its high-performance models in limited numbers, making them exclusive and sought after by enthusiasts.
