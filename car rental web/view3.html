BENTELY

The Bentley Flying Spur is a luxurious sedan that offers a stunning combination of performance, refinement, and technology. Here are some of its key specifications:

    *Engines:*

  • Standard: 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 with 542 hp and 770 Nm torque (0-60 mph in 4.1 seconds, top speed of 198 mph)
  • Hybrid: 2.9L twin-turbocharged V6 with 416 hp and 457 lb-ft torque, combined with a 100 kW electric motor for a total output of 536 hp and 553 lb-ft torque (0-60 mph in 4.1 seconds, top speed of 177 mph, electric range up to 25 miles)
  • W12 (Flying Spur Speed):* 6.0L twin-turbocharged W12 with 626 hp and 900 Nm torque (0-60 mph in 3.7 seconds, top speed of 207 mph)
  • Transmission: All engines are paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.
